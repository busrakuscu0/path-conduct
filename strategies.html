STRATEGIC SELECTION

The Prioritizer

Filter your tasks to separate daily busywork from what actually moves the needle. Choose this if you feel overwhelmed and need to focus on true priorities.

Deep Focus Sprints

Break your work into intense 25-minute intervals followed by short breaks. Choose this if you easily get distracted and want to build steady momentum.

Quick Wins

Instantly execute any new task that takes less than two minutes to complete. Choose this if you feel stuck and need a fast way to clear your mental RAM.

Tunnel Vision

Lock in specific, dedicated time blocks for single tasks. Choose this if you are constantly multitasking and need pure, uninterrupted execution.
