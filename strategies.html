STRATEGIC SELECTION

Time Blocking

Schedule dedicated time blocks for specific tasks to eliminate context switching. Select this strategy to stop multitasking and force deep, uninterrupted focus throughout your workflow.

Eisenhower Matrix

Filter your tasks into quadrants based on strict urgency and importance. Select this strategy if you feel overwhelmed by daily busywork and need to prioritize what truly drives progress.

Pomodoro Sprints

Work in intense 25-minute intervals followed by short 5-minute mental resets. Select this strategy to easily beat distractions, maintain steady energy, and prevent mental burnout.

The 2-Minute Rule

Instantly execute any new task that takes less than two minutes to complete. Select this strategy to stop procrastinating on small details and quickly clear your mental RAM.
